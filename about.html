About HearMe

MAKING THE WORLD A BETTER PLACE FOR ALL OF US

OUR MISSION

At HearMe, we provide a safe space for students to understand and manage their mental health. Our platform offers a self-assessment quiz to help you recognize signs of stress, anxiety, or other challenges. We also provide helpful resources on common mental health issues, practical coping strategies, and ways to seek support. Whether you need quick tips, encouragement, or someone to talk to, we’re here to help.

We integrate humanity with science by using mental health education, an evidence-based method, to empower people’s understanding of their own and others’ physical and mental wellbeing. Through lectures, discussions, and practice people build up a repertoire of positive coping mechanisms, rendering them better at dealing with current stressors and more resilient to future ones.

OUR VISION

HearMe envisions a world where mental health education is integrated into all community centers, schools and companies with the aim to prevent and reduce mental illness. This vision is two-fold: HearMe envisions a world where an individual has the general knowledge to understand their feelings, thoughts and sensations from a personal and scientific perspective. In parallel, we envision a world where the community (friends, family, community centers) can serve as reliable support for individuals struggling with mental illness. HearMe envisions this world to rely on evidence-based methods, including technology, education, and group-activities, to continuously support individuals and communities thrive.

OUR STRATEGY

We focus on awareness, assessment, and support to help students navigate mental health challenges effectively. Awareness – We provide clear, accessible information about common mental health issues, breaking the stigma around seeking help. Assessment – Our self-assessment quiz helps students recognize early signs of stress, anxiety, and other challenges, encouraging self-reflection. Support – We offer practical coping strategies and connect students with helpful resources to guide them toward better mental well-being.

OUR MANAGEMENT

Our team is made up of dedicated individuals working together to create a platform that supports student mental health. Each member brings unique skills and expertise to ensure the project is impactful and effective. Together, we are committed to building a platform that makes mental health support accessible, informative, and impactful.

OUR IMPACT

We aim to make a real difference in how students understand and manage their mental health. Raising Awareness – Providing clear, accessible information on mental health challenges to reduce stigma. Encouraging Self-Reflection – Helping students recognize early signs of stress, anxiety, and other challenges through our self-assessment quiz. Providing Practical Support – Offering coping strategies, helpful resources, and direct access to support services.
